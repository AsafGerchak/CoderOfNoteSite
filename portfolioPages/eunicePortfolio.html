Portfolio Piece

Eunice Bae: Fine Artist

A custom-built WordPress theme, made to replace the current website of artist Eunice Bae. She's also the office manager at HackerYou, so I wanted to do something nice for her. This site provides an elegant and attractive way to showcase her work, and uses WordPress' back-end to allow her to easily update the site with new work whenver she wants.

Languages

HTML, CSS3, JavaScript, PHP.

Tools

Sass, jQuery, Grunt, WordPress.

Bonus points

Easy client-side updates, modern design, responsive across desktop and mobile.
